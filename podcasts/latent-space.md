## Why It Matters

Where Dwarkesh asks "will AI change the world?", Latent Space asks **"how do I build the caching layer for Anthropic's API next week?"** — the latter is far more useful for engineers.

**swyx's unique asset:** He founded the AI Engineer conference series, knows every CTO in San Francisco's AI startup ecosystem. Guests are almost exclusively hands-on engineers from OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, Databricks — not CEOs or VCs.

Conversations go straight to the details: "what model, what token price, what latency, what pitfalls."

### The Annual Report

Every year they publish the **"State of AI Engineering"** report — the de facto industry benchmark for the AI engineering field. If you want to know where AI infrastructure is heading, this is the single most actionable document published each year.

## Essential Episodes

- **State of AI Engineering (annual special)** — The year's most comprehensive review of the AI engineering landscape
- **Agent infrastructure deep-dives** — Any episode on agent frameworks, RAG systems, or inference optimization
- **OpenAI / Anthropic / Meta engineers** — Direct from the teams building the infrastructure
