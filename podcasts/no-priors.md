# No Priors

> Silicon Valley's top VC partners let you sit in on their portfolio meetings. 30–45 minutes, maximum density.

## Info

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hosts** | Sarah Guo (Conviction VC founder, former Greylock partner) + Elad Gil (serial entrepreneur, 40+ unicorn investor) |
| **Focus** | AI startup investing, Silicon Valley strategy |
| **Format** | Interview + discussion, 30–45 min |
| **Release** | Weekly |
| **Platform** | YouTube / Apple / Spotify |

## Why It Matters

Sarah Guo is one of the most active AI investors in Silicon Valley. Elad Gil has backed over 40 unicorns. Together, they produce interviews that are effectively **a window into top-tier VC partner meetings** — the questions they ask are the same ones they use to decide whether to write a $50 million check.

### Guest Caliber

The guest list matches: Jensen Huang (NVIDIA), Andrej Karpathy, Fei-Fei Li, Alexandr Wang (Scale AI), Bret Taylor (former Salesforce co-CEO). These people usually give methodology talks. Sarah and Elad get them to talk **tactics** — like Jensen detailing NVIDIA's internal GPU allocation logic.

### The Format Advantage

At 30–45 minutes per episode, No Priors hits the **sweet spot** for information density vs. time investment. Shorter than Dwarkesh (3 hours) and Cognitive Revolution (2 hours), denser than the news podcasts. Perfect for a single commute.

## Essential Episodes

- **Jensen Huang** — NVIDIA's GPU strategy, supply chain, and where compute is heading
- **Andrej Karpathy** — His view on AI education, Tesla experience, and the future of AI-native companies
- **Fei-Fei Li** — Spatial intelligence, World Labs vision, and the next wave of AI

## Best For

- Anyone who wants Silicon Valley insider perspective without the time commitment of 3-hour podcasts
- People interested in the business and investment side of AI
