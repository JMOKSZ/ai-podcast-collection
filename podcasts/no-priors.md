## 🇨🇳 中文简介

Sarah Guo 是硅谷最活跃的 AI 投资人之一，Elad Gil 投过的独角兽超过 40 家。两人一起做的访谈相当于带你旁听硅谷顶级 VC 的內部会议——他们问的问题，是真的在判断"这家公司值不值得投 5000 万美金"。嘉宾包括 Jensen Huang（NVIDIA CEO）、Andrej Karpathy、李飞飞、Alexandr Wang（Scale AI 创始人）、Bret Taylor 等——全是这个量级的人物。单集 30–45 分钟，比 Dwarkesh 短得多，通勤一趟正好听完。对于追求英文播客的入门者，No Priors 是密度和长度比最好的选择。

## Why It Matters

Sarah Guo is one of the most active AI investors in Silicon Valley. Elad Gil has backed over 40 unicorns. When they interview someone together, it's like eavesdropping on a **top-tier VC committee meeting** — the questions they ask are the ones that determine "should I invest $50M in this company?"

Their guest list is equally impressive: **Jensen Huang, Andrej Karpathy, Fei-Fei Li, Alexandr Wang, Bret Taylor**. These people usually talk about methodology in public interviews, but Sarah and Elad get them to talk about specific tactics — like Jensen's episode where he explained NVIDIA's internal GPU allocation logic.

### Why It's Great for Newcomers

At 30–45 minutes per episode, No Priors has the **best density-to-length ratio** among English AI podcasts. If you can only listen during commutes, this is the one to start with.

## Essential Episodes

- **Jensen Huang (NVIDIA CEO)** — GPU allocation strategy, AI infrastructure vision
- **Andrej Karpathy** — AI education, Tesla learnings, his take on where we're heading
- **Fei-Fei Li** — Computer vision history, World Labs, spatial intelligence
