## 🇨🇳 中文简介

这档播客的独特之处在于 Nathan Labenz 的个人经历——他参与过 GPT-4 的内部红队测试，即 OpenAI 在 GPT-4 发布前找他和一批人内测找漏洞。这段经历让他在 AI 安全和能力边界议题上有第一手认知。但真正让他出圈的是他用 AI 帮助患癌儿子做就医决策的那期节目，把"AI 应用"从工程话题拉到"真实生活决策"的层面，是英文 AI 播客圈公认的现象级单集。如果你想了解 AI 不仅是工具、而是生活伙伴的可能性，这是英文圈做得最深的一档。

## Why It Matters

Nathan's unique advantage is his GPT-4 internal red team experience — OpenAI brought him and a select group to stress-test GPT-4 before public release. This gives him first-hand knowledge of AI safety boundaries and capabilities that most podcast hosts simply don't have.

But what made Cognitive Revolution truly break out was Nathan's deeply personal episode about using AI to make medical decisions for his son's cancer treatment. That episode elevated AI from engineering talk to **real-life decision making** — widely considered a landmark moment in the AI podcast space.

He has since produced several follow-up episodes on AI in healthcare, education, and family decision-making.

## Essential Episodes

- **Using AI for son's cancer treatment** — The episode that defined the show. AI as a life partner, not a tool.
- **Bolt.new founder interview** — Building AI products that people actually use
- **AI in healthcare & education** — Practical, human-centered AI applications
