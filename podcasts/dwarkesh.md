## Why It Matters

Dwarkesh is the best-prepared interviewer in the English AI podcast space — period.

Before every episode, he reads every paper the guest has ever published, every internal report they've written, every public interview from the past 5 years. Then he walks in with 80 hyper-specific questions. This means guests have no room for talking points or PR scripts — he only asks what they haven't said before.

The result is guest quality and depth that no other podcast matches:

- **Dario Amodei** (Anthropic CEO) — recorded twice in one year, including a dedicated episode on the "AI 2027" scenario
- **Demis Hassabis** (Google DeepMind CEO) — came on right after Gemini 3 launch
- **Mira Murati** — her first long-form interview after leaving OpenAI and founding Thinking Machines Lab
- **Andrej Karpathy** — multiple appearances
- **Ilya Sutskever** — appeared after leaving OpenAI
- **Carl Shulman** — the nearly 6-hour episode that's considered a must-listen across the entire industry

## How to Start

**Warning:** His pace is fast and dense. This is not a commute podcast. Set aside dedicated time.

### Essential Episodes

- **Carl Shulman** (~6 hours) — The most frequently cited episode in AI safety and capabilities discussions. Covers takeoff speeds, compute trends, and alignment difficulty. Industry consensus: must-listen.
- **Dario Amodei** — Anthropic's CEO on the "2027" scenario, safety strategy, and why Anthropic exists.
- **Mira Murati** — Her first deep dive after leaving OpenAI. Insight into OpenAI's internal culture and the Thinking Machines Lab vision.
- **Demis Hassabis** — Gemini 3 strategy, AlphaFold, and DeepMind's approach to AGI.

## Best For

- Serious AI researchers, engineers, and investors
- Anyone who wants the raw signal, not the PR-filtered version
- People willing to invest 3–5 hours for knowledge that's worth 10+ news articles

## When Not to Listen

- While driving (too dense, requires focus)
- When you're tired (you'll miss the key points)
- If you're brand new to AI (start with No Priors or Cognitive Revolution first)
